About Us

We are an indie game development team. We started from one but have grown to many. We are producing a massively open world game that we hope with your help that we will become #1.

How we started

I grew tired of failing teams due to a lack of members. So I decided to post an advert on IndieDB to see if people wanted to join. Before I knew it my inbox blew up with people wanting to join. The team grew, with memebers joining and even some leaving, we are prodcuing a game that I never thought would be possible. These new team mates made everything possible. They are a talented group of people.

- Seth E.

The future

The future holds a team, that with time, will grow both in numbers and in skills/experience. With determination and persistence, we believe that in time, we could build a company that is not only recognised for creating great games but also for being a great place and atmosphere to make games.
